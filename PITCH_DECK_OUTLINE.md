## Presentation Tips

### Timing (5-10 minute pitch)
- **Problem & Solution**: 2 minutes
- **Demo/Features**: 3-4 minutes
- **Tech Stack & Innovation**: 1-2 minutes
- **Impact & Future**: 1-2 minutes
- **Q&A**: Remaining time

### Key Points to Emphasize
1. **AI Innovation**: Highlight the computer vision aspect
2. **Real-World Impact**: Show actual civic problems solved
3. **Completeness**: Full-stack solution, not just an idea
4. **User Experience**: Easy for citizens, powerful for admins
5. **Scalability**: Can be deployed in any city

### Demo Flow (If Live Demo)
1. Open user app
2. Capture photo of issue (or use sample)
3. Show AI detection result
4. Submit complaint
5. Switch to admin dashboard
6. Show issue in dashboard
7. Update status
8. Show notification received

### Backup Plan (If Demo Fails)
- Pre-recorded video demo
- Screenshots with annotations
- Step-by-step walkthrough
